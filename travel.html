Getting to us.

MAP

VENUE

The wedding will be held at Harefield Barn. Just outside Exceter. The address is:

Harefield Barn, Shute, Shobrooke, Crediton, Devon, EX17 1BW

Public Transport

If you wish to arrive by public trasnport your nearest train station is Exeter which is 15 minutes away from the venue. You will then need to use local taxi firms to finish your journey as there is no public transport near the venue.

If flying is more your thing then your nearest airport is Exeter which has routes from many UK regional airports.

TAXIS

DRIVING

Be warned, if you are using sat-nav, the postcode covers the whole village and will take you to its center. The venue is clearly signposted from the road. If you are arriving from Exeter or the south using the postcode it is the first turning on the right once you crossed the staggered junction. If you are arriving from the north go all the way through Shute it is the last left after you pass the farm enterance. If you get to a staggered junction then you've gone too far.

There is plenty of parking onsite for everyone at the venue.

The address of the venue is Harefield Barn, Shute, Shobrooke, Crediton EX17 1BW
