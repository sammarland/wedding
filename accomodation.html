Places to stay.

COTTAGES

There are a variaty of local Pubs that have rooms available. There are also private lets available on websites such as HomeAway and Airbnb. If you aren't sure about the area please get in contact with us at our wedding email here.

HOTELS

There are a good selection of hotels available in Exeter which is a 15 minute drive from the venue. We have looked on Hotels.com and Trivago.com, but as As we haven't stayed in any of the local hotels ourselves we don't feel that we can recommmend any in particular. However we can answer questions about different areas and how easy it is to get to the venue.

OTHER IDEAS

For those of you brave enough, there will be plenty of space for camping around the venue. If anyone does camp we will provide you with a warm breakfast to thaw you out.

If you have a campervan or carvan, then we can provide you a space at the venue to pitch up overnight.

There are 3 local glamping sites that we've found on GoGlamping.com
